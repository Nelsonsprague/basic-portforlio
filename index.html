About Me

My Cup of Tea Meaning: Someone or something that one finds to be agreeable or delightful. Put a Sock In It Meaning: Asking someone to be quiet or to shut up. Tough It Out Meaning: To remain resillient even in hard times; enduring. Lickety Split Meaning: To go at a quick pace; no delaying! If You Can't Stand the Heat, Get Out of the Kitchen Meaning: One should discontinue with a task if they are unable to cope with it due to pressure. Money Doesn't Grow On Trees Meaning: Suggests that money is a resource that must be earned and is not one that's easily acquired. Break The Ice Meaning: Breaking down a social stiffness. Talk the Talk Meaning: Supporting what you say, not just with words, but also through action or evidence. Ugly Duckling Meaning: One who may seem plain at first in appearance or capability, but later turns out to be beautiful or great. Hit Below The Belt Meaning: A boxing term. Also often used to refer to inappropriate words, or comments that are too personal.

Happy as a Clam Meaning: The state of being happy; feeling delighted. Scot-free Meaning: Getting away freely from custody, punishment, or any type of risky situation. Quality Time Meaning: Spending time with another to strengthen the relationship. Fight Fire With Fire Meaning: To retaliate with an attack that is similar to the attack used against you. On Cloud Nine Meaning: Having strong feelings of happiness or satisfaction. Keep Your Shirt On Meaning: Keeping calm. Usually said by someone who is trying to avoid making others upset. Cry Over Spilt Milk Meaning: It's useless to worry about things that already happened and cannot be changed. Quick On the Draw Meaning: Performing an action with the greatest of haste.
